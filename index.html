🐛 Pest & Disease Guide

Identify common crop threats and find both chemical and organic SFT-approved treatment methods.

Hemiptera

Whiteflies (සුදු මැස්සා)

Symptoms: Leaf curling, Yellowing, Sticky honeydew.

Organic SFT Control

Neem oil spray (කොහොඹ තෙල්), Yellow sticky traps.

Chemical Control

Imidacloprid or Thiamethoxam application.

Tetranychidae

Spider Mites (මයිටාවන්)

Symptoms: Fine webs under leaves, tiny yellow spots.

Organic SFT Control

High pressure water spray, Horticultural oils.

Chemical Control

Abamectin or Wettable Sulfur.

Thysanoptera

Thrips (පැළ මැක්කන්)

Symptoms: Silvering of leaves, distorted and scarred fruits.

Organic SFT Control

Blue sticky traps, Spinosad (Biological).

Chemical Control

Fipronil or Profenofos.
